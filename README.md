# 🚀 Careers at Romangic

> **We're building AI-powered products that reimagine creativity and wellness.**

Romangic is a Beijing-based startup with two product lines serving global users:

## Our Products

### 📸 WayShot — AI Photographer Agent
The world's first AI photographer agent. WayShot handles the entire photography workflow — from scene understanding to composition, camera control, and post-processing. Think of it as your personal professional photographer, powered by AI.

### 🔮 WooMoo — AI Spiritual Companion
An AI-powered tarot and spiritual wellness app for modern seekers. WooMoo goes beyond tools to become a true companion — blending astrology, tarot, and psychology with deep empathy.
